— How it works

Three weeks, three milestones.

Discover runs as a focused sprint. Two senior engineers embedded with your team for ~10 hours a week of meetings, plus async work between. No 6-month consulting engagements. No "let us shadow you for a quarter."

Week 01 — Discovery
Stakeholder interviews + workflow capture

8–15 conversations across operations, eng leadership, and one exec sponsor. We sit in on real workflows. We watch where time gets burned. We ask "what's the dumbest part of this job?" until we get honest answers.

Stakeholder map Live shadowing Tooling audit Existing data review
Week 02 — Synthesis
Workflow mapping + AI feasibility scoring

Every observed workflow gets mapped, scored on AI feasibility (frontier model? RAG? agent? deterministic code?), and stress-tested for ROI. We separate the "this looks AI-shaped" from the "this is actually AI-shaped."

Final week is execution-ready output. Roadmap, architecture for the top pick, ROI on the top three, and a fixed-scope build estimate if you want to keep going. Live read-out to your team in week three.

— Common questions

Things people actually ask.

What if you tell us not to build anything?
Then we say so. Roughly 18% of Discover engagements end with a "don't build this yet" verdict — usually because the data isn't ready, the workflow isn't actually AI-shaped, or there's a deterministic fix that's faster. You still get every artifact. The engagement is worth that answer alone.
How much of our team's time does Discover cost?
Plan for ~10 hours/week of meetings during the sprint, distributed across 8–15 stakeholders. Most individual contributors give us 2–3 hours total. The exec sponsor gives us a kickoff (60 min), a mid-sprint check-in (30 min), and the read-out (90 min).
Do we have to build with you afterward?
No. The roadmap, architecture, and ROI models are yours to keep regardless. About 70% of Discover clients move into Build with us — but it's not a requirement, and we'd rather you take the work to a better-fit partner than build with us out of inertia.
What if we already know what we want to build?
Skip Discover and go straight to Build. We'll scope it, give you a fixed price, and start. Discover is for when you're choosing between options, not when the choice is already made.
Will you sign an NDA before we share anything sensitive?
Always. Mutual NDA before any workflow review or data access. We work in environments you control — we don't take exports of customer data home with us.
