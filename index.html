— FREQUENTLY ASKED

Things people actually ask.

How fast can you actually ship something?
First demo within 2 weeks of kickoff. Full production AI feature in 4–8 weeks for most Build engagements. Discover roadmaps deliver in 2–3 weeks. The speed comes from senior engineers who use Cursor and Claude Code as a force multiplier — not from cutting corners.
What's your typical project size?
Engagements range from short Discover sprints to multi-month Build programs to ongoing Operate retainers. Every engagement is fixed-scope and fixed-price, so you know exactly what you're paying before we start. Share your budget on the contact form and we'll tell you what's possible inside it.
Do you sign NDAs?
Always. We sign mutual NDAs before any sensitive discussions and standard MSA + SOW agreements before kickoff. All client data stays in environments you control or in dedicated infrastructure we set up for your engagement.
Where are you based?
Crestics LLC is registered in Texas, with our studio based in Austin. We work with clients across the US and UK in their preferred timezones.
What AI tools and stacks do you use?
Frontier models from OpenAI, Anthropic, and open-source where appropriate. Voice via Vapi, Retell, or Bland. Custom RAG pipelines on Pinecone/pgvector. Frontend in Next.js. Backend in TypeScript or Python. We pick stacks based on what ships best for your specific use case. Never tool-first.
What if we need to stop the engagement?
Build engagements are fixed-scope so they end naturally at delivery. Operate retainers have a 30-day exit clause. No penalty, no questions. We'd rather you leave when it stops being valuable than stay because you're locked in.
